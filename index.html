GPA CALCULATOR

Welcome to my website, where calculating your academic performance has never been easier! I understand the importance of keeping track of your grades, and that's why i created this tool to help you calculate your SGPA and CGPA effortlessly.

SGPA (Semester Grade Point Average) is a measure of your academic performance in a single semester, whereas CGPA (Cumulative Grade Point Average) reflects your overall academic performance throughout your academic career.

For SGPA CALCULATION

Enter each subject grade on the left side and enter the corresponding subject credit on the right side.

For CGPA CALCULATION

Enter each semester SGPA on the left side and enter the corresponding semester Total credits on right side.

Click CALCULATE to start calculating..... All the best with your result
